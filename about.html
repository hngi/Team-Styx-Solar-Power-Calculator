ABOUT US

Solar Energy is both green and clean, as it has no harmful effects on the environment. In addition, solar power is a stable power source when inverted and excess power is stored to be used later. Solar power is a renewable resource, which means there is so much of it freely available we never have to dig for coal or refine other toxic energy sources just to keep the lights on.


Solar Energy reduces your carbon footprint by decreasing greenhouse gas emissions (which contribute to climate change). Since it does not produce any pollution, it also means we will have less harmful pollutants in our air, land, and water.


Helping users understand the potential benefits of solar power is important to us. Solar power is the way forward for our environment, and in many cases, is financially beneficial for households and businesses.


Styx Energy solar power calculator is independent and free to use; it assists people in making an informed decision about solar systems and number of solar panels to use according to power output per panel to efficiently power the appliances one wants. Solar power calculator is used to calculate and tell you the amount of wattages used on a daily and monthly basis. The calculator helps you calculate the system size you will require, if you wish to install solar panels on your property.


We are a totally customer focused team with the goal to help households, individuals and businesses discover and employ this easy to grasp information to be able to either build or buy the most efficient, cost-effective, safe and aesthetically pleasing solar power system that suits best their energy needs.
